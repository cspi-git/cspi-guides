[ Q&A ]
How can I secure my pc completely with proxies n stuff I don't want to spend a lot but I want to make it really almost untraceable?
Your computer itself is impossible to be 100% anonymous and using a VPN or proxy always on your computer does not really make a difference based on my opinion, instead of making your computer anonymous itself, focus on services, websites and etc online because they are the one that logs information and invades privacy and track you.

But it can be secure; just don't install random software online that may be malware. "Focus on the gate of your house that is protecting you not the inside of the house".
