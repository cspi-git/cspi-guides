Discipline

Does not mean that you have secured your accounts, network or something, your 100% confident that your privacy is ol'good well no. Over the time websites also change their privacy policies, the tools the hackers are using are advancing including the government.

And there is also a risk that you will blunder and accidently expose your identity or personal account that could lead to you, so be careful. What you should do is, every week or regularly stay updated to the updates of the website that you use and check your accounts for possible breaches and if you're determined enough, change your accounts password weekly.

Left Deep Information

Yourself

So when talking to someone, always know the consequences and what it can do before speaking or messaging it out to someone.

Outside Part
