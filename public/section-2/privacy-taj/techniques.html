Misleading your enemy, isn't that really hard, it's all about knowing what they might or will check.

Here is a quick example:
So you are a hacker from a hackers forum but for some reason people hate you because of the way you talk, and somehow you noticed that some people may be trying to dox you, but you already know you have secured your privacy on that subject but want to make it interesting.

Second, they will scrape and look deeply in your profile and stuff like content and possible emails but since you have secured your or this subject, you don't worry about it, because they will find nothing but dead-ends. Now to make it interesting, you can just post some fake information about you in pastes websites that they might visit or other stuff. Well, that is basically it.
